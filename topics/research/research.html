RESEARCH POSITIONS

In the Mechanical Science and Engineering Department I am currently working on multiscale computation for materials science and condensed matter physics. My current research focuses on the tight binding method, which we are coupling to couple to other higher accuracy methods like quantum Monte Carlo via the creation of bespoke parameterizations.

In the Computer Science Department I work on numerical and theoretical aspects of the tensor eigenvalue problem.

April 2021-present: Labaratory for Parallel Numerical Algorithms, Edgar Solomonik, UIUC

July 2020-present: Harley T. Johnson Group, UIUC

The majority of my research as an undergraduate involved phenomena related to nonequilibrium plasmas - from dielectric barrier discharges to plasma jets, and from experimentation and instrumentation to computation.
