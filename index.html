I am a second year Ph.D. student in the Computational Science, Engineering, and Mathematics graduate program in the Oden Institute advised by Joe Kileel.

I graduated from the University of Notre Dame in 2020 with my bachelor’s in Mechanical Engineering, and from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign in 2022 with my master’s in Theoretical and Applied Mechanics.

My primary focus is on computational and applied mathematics, especially numerical analysis, (multi)linear algebra, and optimization. I also enjoy the intersections of these topics with high performance computing, theoretical computer science, and multiscale modeling.
